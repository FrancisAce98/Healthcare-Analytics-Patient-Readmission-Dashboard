As a COTA transitioning into Healthcare Analystics, I analyzed a large dataset of 100k patient records to identify main key factors contributing to re-admissions.

## Key Insights (What I learned) 
Patients in the age bracket (70-80) have the highest volume of re-admissions, totalling 26,000 cases.
There is a significant spike in re-admission for patients that are discharged after a *3-day stay* accounting for about 17% of re-admissions.
## Action 
Focus dicharge planning for 3-day stays, Implement a post-discharge follow-up, home safety evaluation for patients specifically who discharged on day 3.
